Student Developer

Biopragmatics Lab

I contributed to a few Python packages created by my mentor, Dr. Charles Tapley Hoyt, specifically PyBEL and Bio2BEL. For my first project with Dr. Hoyt, I created the pybel.schema module, which added functions to PyBEL to verify the data format of a given entity. In doing so, I helped assist in the integration of heterogenous biological data, a necessary first step for the computational analysis of the mechanisms of complex diseases.
