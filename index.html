Experience

Research Assistant

Flatiron Institute

I'm an intern at the Flatiron Institute's Center for Computational Neuroscience (CCN) in Professor Alex Williams's group. We're leveraging machine learning methods to characterize the vocal repertoire and social behaviors of gerbils in naturalistic environments.

My specific task is to help train deep neural networks to determine the location of a vocalization and assign it to a gerbil using only microphone array data, a project which will allow us to quantify and study sequences of vocalizations—or "conversations" between the gerbils. Currently, I'm working on quantifying uncertainty in the outputs from our CNN and transformer sound source localization models.

June 2022 - August 2022

Research Intern

CUNY Graduate School of Public Health

Along with my mentor, Professor Zach Shahn, I applied causal inference to help doctors better understand the process of weaning patients off mechanical ventilation. Specifically, we used a technique called incremental propensity score intervention to estimate how varying strategies for liberating ICU patients from mechanical ventilation affects outcomes like mortality and ICU-free days. Our paper, for which I’m a co-first author, is currently being reviewed at the Chest journal.

May 2021 - September 2021

Student Developer

Biopragmatics Lab

I contributed to a few Python packages created by my mentor, Dr. Charles Tapley Hoyt, specifically PyBEL and Bio2BEL. For my first project with Dr. Hoyt, I created the pybel.schema module, which added functions to PyBEL to verify the data format of a given entity. Throughout that project, I learned about JSON Schema and best practices for developing a suite of unit tests.

Most recently, I integrated the Rhea database of reactions into Bio2BEL. This enabled reactions stored in the Rhea format to be parsed into Python objects fitting the Biological Expression Language (BEL) specification. In doing so, I helped assist in the integration of heterogenous biological data, a necessary first step for the computational analysis of the mechanisms of complex diseases.
